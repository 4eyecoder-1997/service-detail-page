What actually our carpet cleaning services include?

Inspecting your carpet

We need to meet you and do a little bit of inspection before starting our cleaning work. Our professional hand selected cleaners take precise measurements, note the areas that generally need extra attention and mark spots, stains and high traffic area.

Pulling with rotary machine

Next, we use our advance rotary machine onto the carpet surface which deep scrubs and pulls out the chemicals that were sprayed earlier. Along with the cleaning chemical that we sprayed earlier; oil, dust, grease and other stain chemicals are pulled out by the machine.

Spray carpet cleaning agent

We spray eco-friendly carpet cleaning chemicals over your carpet before the carpet dry cleaning service is started. These chemicals loosen the carpet tension and make it completely ready to be dry cleaned.
