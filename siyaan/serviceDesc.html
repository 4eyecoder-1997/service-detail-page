What is the difference between carpet dry cleaning and steam cleaning in Australia?

Carpet dry cleaning is a process that uses minimal water and is ideal for busy households and offices. Steam cleaning, on the other hand, uses hot water and steam to clean your carpets thoroughly. Both methods are effective and the choice depends on your specific requirements.

How often should I get my carpets cleaned in Australia?

The frequency of carpet cleaning in Australia depends on your specific requirements. For households with pets or small children, it is recommended to clean carpets at least twice a year. For commercial spaces, it may be necessary to clean carpets more frequently.

How long does it take for carpets to dry after steam cleaning in Australia?

The drying time for carpets after steam cleaning in Australia varies depending on the humidity, airflow, and temperature in the room. Typically, it takes between 4-6 hours for carpets to dry completely after steam cleaning.
